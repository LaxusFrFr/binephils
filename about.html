About Bine Philippines Inc.

A legacy of excellence in electroplating and construction

Our Story

Founded on March 8, 2001, Bine Construction and Development Corporation was established with a clear vision: to help build safer and more efficient infrastructure across the Philippines. The company was founded by Flor Bañaga, a registered civil engineer, and her business partner Kyung Mo Kim, with the goal of practicing engineering through meaningful construction and traffic safety solutions.

Mission

Bine Phils. Inc. is committed to building lasting client relationships through honesty, integrity, and consistent delivery of quality solutions; retaining and strengthening client loyalty through service excellence; driving innovation in industrial manufacturing and electroplating; providing reliable construction and traffic safety solutions; improving lives through competitive safety and infrastructure products; developing our people and expanding capabilities for long-term growth; and creating sustainable business value through innovation and responsible operations.

Vision

To become a leading Philippine industrial and infrastructure company recognized for innovation, integrity, and long-term partnerships, and to grow into a Triple-A contractor that contributes to safer communities and national development.

Our Goals

Expand our market presence nationally and internationally; continuously innovate our products and services; foster a culture of excellence and integrity; and contribute to sustainable community development.

Our Commitment

Quality electroplating for electronics and industrial applications; excellence in construction and development; sustainable and eco-friendly practices; and customer satisfaction and safety first.

Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do: respect for people; integrity in business; accountability; commitment to quality; productivity and efficiency; continuous innovation; and exceeding expectations in everything we do.

Bine Philippines Inc.

The company traces its roots to its mother company, Bine Phils Inc., which was originally engaged in jewelry manufacturing. Over time, Bine Phils Inc. evolved its operations and is now focused on industrial electroplating services for the electronics industry, providing specialized surface-finishing solutions for various electronic components. This strong industrial background laid the foundation for the establishment of Bine Construction and Development Corporation.

From the beginning, the company has focused on construction development with a specialization in traffic engineering. What sets the company apart is its commitment to introducing innovative and distinctive products designed to improve road safety and infrastructure performance.

Over the years, the company has primarily worked with government agencies, infrastructure operators, and industrial clients, providing solutions for traffic safety systems and solar-powered infrastructure. Among its key accomplishments are projects involving solar street lighting systems, solar-powered bus sheds, and various traffic safety installations.

Through dedication, integrity, and strong partnerships, the company has successfully completed more than 100 projects nationwide, including its largest project—the Improvement and Rehabilitation of Roads in Kalinga–Apayao. The trust of clients such as the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA), North Luzon Expressway (NLEX), South Luzon Expressway (SLEX), the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH), and various municipalities reflects the company’s commitment to quality and reliability.

Today, the company operates from Golden Mile Business Park in Carmona, Cavite, serving clients nationwide. With a team of engineers, managers, and skilled personnel totaling around 50 employees, the company continues to deliver services in general construction, project management, and specialized traffic engineering solutions. The company is also a PCAB-licensed contractor (Small B category) with relevant certifications and accreditations.

As the company continues to grow, it remains guided by its founding purpose: to apply engineering expertise to real-world infrastructure challenges. Looking ahead, the company aims to develop new technologies and products, expand its client base, and continue supporting the country’s infrastructure development through innovative and reliable solutions.

Bine Philippines Inc.
