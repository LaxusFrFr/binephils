Our Story

BINE Philippines, Inc. is a diversified engineering and manufacturing company with over two decades of experience in industrial solutions and infrastructure development. Established in 1996, the company has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality products and services to both local and international markets.

Originally founded as a fashion jewelry manufacturer, BINE Philippines provided end-to-end production services—from design and casting to electroplating and final assembly. Through partnerships with global brands such as Avon, the company developed deep expertise in precision metal finishing and advanced plating technologies.

Leveraging its technical foundation, BINE Philippines expanded into industrial applications through its Industrial Division, specializing in:

  • Electroplating
  • Electroless plating

This division serves a wide range of industries requiring precision coating, enhanced durability, and high-performance materials.

In 2001, the company further diversified with the establishment of BINE Construction and Development Corporation, strengthening its capabilities in construction, infrastructure, and traffic engineering. This expansion marked a strategic move into public and private sector development projects.

Construction and Infrastructure

The Construction Division has successfully completed over 100 projects nationwide, working with key government agencies and major infrastructure operators, including:

  • Metropolitan Manila Development Authority (MMDA)
  • North Luzon Expressway (NLEX)
  • South Luzon Expressway (SLEX)
  • Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH)
  • Department of Transportation (DOTr)

Core services include:

  • Traffic engineering and safety solutions
  • Solar-powered infrastructure (street lighting, bus sheds)
  • General construction and project management

Notable projects include large-scale road improvement initiatives in Kalinga–Apayao, as well as nationwide installations of solar lighting and traffic safety systems.
