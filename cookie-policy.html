Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Bine Philippines Inc. ("Bine", "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on this website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember information such as preferences, session details, and usage patterns.

How do we use Cookies?

Essential and form-related cookies: When you use our contact and quote forms, certain cookies may be used to maintain session stability, prevent duplicate submissions, and preserve limited input context during your visit.

Performance and analytics cookies: We may use analytics tools to understand general website usage, such as pages viewed and time spent per section. This information is evaluated in aggregate to improve usability and content quality, and is not used by us to directly identify you as an individual.

What are your options with cookies?

You may control cookies through your browser settings. Depending on your browser, you can accept all cookies, block specific categories, clear existing cookies, or receive notifications before a cookie is stored.

If you disable cookies, some website features may not function properly, including portions of form-related behavior and overall browsing convenience.

To change browser cookie settings, you may visit:

  • Google Chrome
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Apple Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
