Services

Precision plating and finishing solutions for electric and industrial components.

Electroplating

We offer a wide range of electroplating solutions designed for both functional and decorative applications.

Copper Plating

High-conductivity copper deposits for electronics, providing excellent adhesion and uniform coverage on complex substrates.

Nickel Plating

Durable nickel coatings offering superior corrosion resistance and wear protection for industrial components.

Tin Plating

Reliable tin deposits ensuring excellent solderability, corrosion resistance, and whisker-free finishes.

Palladium Plating

Precision palladium coatings for connectors and contacts requiring high reliability and stable contact resistance.

Rhodium Plating

Ultra-hard rhodium finishes providing exceptional wear resistance and low contact resistance for critical applications.

Gold Plating

High-purity gold deposits delivering superior conductivity and long-term reliability for precision electronics.

Electroless Plating

Consistent coating thickness and excellent surface coverage, even on complex geometries.
